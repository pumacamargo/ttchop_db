# PRODUCT DESCRIPTION: ELLISS LIPOSOMAL VITAMIN C 1000MG

### PRODUCT NAME:
ELLISS Liposomal Vitamin C 1000mg (Lemon Flavor)

### PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
This is a high-potency, premium oral beauty supplement featuring 1,000mg of 100% liposomal Vitamin C per serving. Formulated under the strict supervision of cosmetic dermatologists and certified as a Food with Nutrient Function Claims (栄養機能食品) in Japan, this high-absorption powder is carefully manufactured without thermal processing to protect the delicate properties of Vitamin C. It blends advanced delivery science with luxurious natural ingredients to cultivate a radiant, glow-from-within complexion.

### HOW IT IS USED:
As a daily nutritional and beauty supplement, users take 1 convenient powder packet (sachet) per day. The powder can be taken directly by mouth thanks to its refreshing lemon taste, or smoothly mixed into a glass of water or lukewarm water depending on individual preference.

### MAIN BENEFITS:

* **Maximum Liposomal Absorption:** Utilizing a 100% liposomal encapsulation structure that significantly enhances absorption efficiency and prevents the Vitamin C from being easily flushed out of the body.
* **Advanced Dermatologist Supervision:** Developed alongside cosmetic skin care doctors, ensuring an optimal concentration of active ingredients designed directly for premium inner skin serums and wellness care.
* **Rich Botanical Beauty Blend:** Powerfully enriched with handpicked aesthetic extracts, including luxurious aloe vera, resveratrol, citrus extract, alpha-lipoic acid, lycopene, and Asian ginseng to fight oxidation and dullness.
* **Heat-Free Fresh Powder Form:** Processed using an advanced non-thermal manufacturing technique that preserves the total freshness and high activity level of the vitamins in a delicious, easy-to-carry lemon sachet.

### VISUAL APPEARANCE:

* **Size:** Each retail box contains 30 individual powder packets (sachets), providing a complete 30-day supply.
* **Shape:** Slim, lightweight, stick-type single-use powder pouches packed within a premium, minimalist outer box.
* **Color:** The packaging features a clean, high-end white and yellow cosmetic design that mirrors its refreshing lemon flavor.
* **How it is typically held or used:** Easily torn open by hand with zero hassle, allowing the powder to be smoothly consumed anywhere—whether at home, work, or while traveling.

### RESTRICTIONS:

* **Adhere to Daily Guidelines:** Users should consume the standard recommendation of 1 packet per day and avoid excessive intake.
* **Allergy and Sensitivity Check:** Individuals with known ingredient sensitivities, pregnant women, or nursing mothers should review the formulation and consult a doctor if necessary.
* **Storage and Handling Care:** To protect the delicate liposomal powder texture, store the packets in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, high heat, and humidity.

## JAPAN COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES (PMD ACT / 薬機法)

### WHAT YOU CAN SAY (PERMITTED CLAIMS):
* State that it is officially certified as a "Food with Nutrient Function Claims" (栄養機能食品) in Japan.
* Mention that the formula is completely supervised and developed alongside real Japanese "cosmetic dermatologists" (美容皮膚科医監修).
* Describe the advanced product delivery science: "100% Liposomal encapsulation structure" that enhances absorption efficiency.
* Highlight the premium "non-thermal manufacturing process" (熱を加えない特別な加工) used to preserve the delicate activity of Vitamin C.
* Emphasize general beauty maintenance expressions, such as supporting "inner skincare," "skin radiance," and a "glow-from-within complexion" (飲む美容液 / インナーセラム).

### WHAT YOU CANNOT SAY (FORBIDDEN CLAIMS):
* DO NOT claim it can cure, treat, or prevent skin conditions or physical diseases (e.g., "cures severe acne," "heals dermatitis," or "prevents skin aging").
* DO NOT state that it will medically remove, erase, or prevent melasma and dark spots (e.g., シミを消す / シミの予防). 
* DO NOT promise explicit medical skin whitening or bleaching effects (美白効果). Skincare supplements cannot make therapeutic drug-level claims regarding permanent skin tone modification under the Japanese PMD Act.
