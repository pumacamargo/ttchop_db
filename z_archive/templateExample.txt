VIDEO TEMPLATE BASE

This template defines the visual style, environment, creator behavior, pacing, dialogue style, and output structure of the video.

The creator is always the primary subject.

The product supports the story but should not replace the creator as the main focus.

The video should feel authentic, realistic, and native to social media rather than a traditional advertisement.

VISUAL GUIDELINES

- Keep the creator visible during most of the video.
- Use realistic camera movement.
- Use natural eye contact and believable body language.
- Keep the environment visually consistent.
- Integrate the product naturally into the creator's activity.
- Prefer simple visual sequences appropriate for short-form content.
- Product shots should support what the creator is saying.

DIALOGUE GUIDELINES

- Dialogue should sound conversational and natural.
- The creator should speak like a real person sharing a personal experience.
- Avoid corporate language, sales language, and feature dumping.
- Focus on a single idea, problem, discovery, or outcome.
- Keep dialogue short enough for the intended video duration.

REALISM GUIDELINES

- Favor realism over marketing.
- Avoid exaggerated reactions.
- Avoid unnatural behavior.
- Avoid unrealistic demonstrations.
- Avoid obvious advertising language.

OUTPUT

The template may define its own output structure.

If an output format is explicitly defined, follow it exactly.

If no output format is defined, generate the format that best matches the template requirements.

FINAL GOAL

Create a realistic creator-driven video where the creator remains the main focus and the product is naturally integrated into the story.
