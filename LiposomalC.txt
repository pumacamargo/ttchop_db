# PRODUCT DESCRIPTION: POTENIA POTASSIUM CHLORIDE 1200

### PRODUCT NAME:
POTENIA Potassium Chloride 1200 (With Vitamin C)

### PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
This is a premium, Japanese-made nutritional supplement formulated to support body fluid balance and inner beauty. Each daily serving provides a high concentration of 1,200mg of potassium chloride paired with Vitamin C. It is dual-supervised by medical doctors and registered dietitians to ensure a clean, high-quality formula completely free from sugars, preservatives, artificial colorings, flavorings, and gluten.

### HOW IT IS USED:
As a daily dietary supplement, users take the small, easy-to-swallow tablets with water. The standard fully-supported dosage is 9 tablets per day (delivering 1,200mg of potassium) for a 30-day routine, or it can be adapted into a lighter 3-tablets-per-day maintenance routine spanning up to 90 days.

### MAIN BENEFITS:

* **Dual-Action Cleanse & Beauty Care:** Combines high-potency potassium to help flush out excess cellular salt build-up with Vitamin C to support natural skin radiance and clarity from within.
* **"Panpan" Tightness Solution:** Specially designed for individuals who experience a heavy, tight, or "stuffed" feeling in their limbs after standing all day or due to a lack of fresh vegetables in their diet.
* **Doctor & Dietitian Supervised:** Formulated under strict expert oversight, guaranteeing a perfectly balanced, clean nutritional supplement without any unnecessary chemical additives.
* **Domestic Quality Assurance:** Manufactured entirely in Japan within a domestic GMP-certified factory, ensuring the highest standards of safety, purity, and processing control.

### VISUAL APPEARANCE:

* **Size:** Available in multi-size pouch options, including a standard 270-tablet bag (89.4g) and a compact 90-tablet trial size.
* **Shape:** Small, smooth, round dietary tablets specifically designed to be significantly smaller and much easier to swallow than traditional, bulky overseas vitamins.
* **Color:** Clean, light-toned natural supplement tablets presented in a professional, minimalist resealable eco-pouch.
* **How it is typically held or used:** The lightweight resealable pouch is easily held in one hand, allowing users to neatly dispense a few tablets at a time at home, or carry it in a bag for use at work.

### RESTRICTIONS:

* **Adhere to Recommended Intake:** Users should follow the daily consumption guidelines and avoid taking more than the recommended daily dose.
* **Consult Before Use:** Pregnant women, nursing mothers, or individuals currently taking other medications should consult with a healthcare professional before adding it to their routine.
* **Dietary Complement Only:** This supplement is intended to support an active lifestyle and should be used alongside a varied, well-balanced diet and proper hydration.

## JAPAN COMPLIANCE GUIDELINES (PMD ACT / 薬機法)

### WHAT YOU CAN SAY (PERMITTED CLAIMS):
* Use the official certification: "Food with Nutrient Function Claims" (栄養機能食品).
* Describe the physical target using the brand's safe terminology: Helps with the "heavy, tight, or stuffed feeling" (Panpan / パンパン対策).
* Highlight that it supports "body fluid and salt balance" or "inner beauty and freshness".
* Mention that the manufacturing plant is domestically certified under Japanese "GMP standards".
* State that the formula is completely supervised by real Japanese medical doctors and registered dietitians.

### WHAT YOU CANNOT SAY (FORBIDDEN CLAIMS):
* DO NOT use medical terms like "cures edema" or "removes swelling" (むくみを治す/解消する). Edema is considered a medical condition under Japanese law.
* DO NOT claim it works as a weight-loss pill or treats any physical disease.
* DO NOT state that Vitamin C will "cure spots" or medically "whiten the skin" (シミを消す/美白効果).
