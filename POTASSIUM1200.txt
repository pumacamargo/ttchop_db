# PRODUCT DESCRIPTION: POTENIA POTASSIUM CHLORIDE 1200

### PRODUCT NAME:
POTENIA Potassium Chloride 1200 (With Vitamin C)

### PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
This is a premium, Japanese-made nutritional supplement formulated to support body fluid balance and inner beauty. Classified as a certified Food with Nutrient Function Claims (栄養機能食品), each daily serving provides a high concentration of 1,200mg of potassium chloride paired with Vitamin C. It is dual-supervised by medical doctors and registered dietitians to ensure a clean, high-quality formula free from sugars, preservatives, artificial colorings, and flavorings.

### HOW IT IS USED:
As a dietary supplement, users typically take the small, easy-to-swallow tablets with water. The standard fully-supported dosage is 9 tablets per day (delivering 1,200mg of potassium) for a 30-day routine, or it can be adapted into a lighter 3-tablets-per-day maintenance routine spanning up to 90 days.

### MAIN BENEFITS:

* **Dual-Action Cleanse & Beauty Care:** Combines potassium to help flush out excess cellular salt build-up with Vitamin C to support natural skin radiance and clarity from within.
* **"Panpan" Tightness Solution:** Specially designed for individuals who experience a heavy, tight, or "stuffed" feeling in their limbs after standing all day or due to a lack of fresh vegetables in their diet.
* **Doctor & Dietitian Supervised:** Formulated under strict expert oversight, guaranteeing a gluten-free and clean nutritional balance without any unnecessary chemical additives.
* **Domestic Quality Assurance:** Manufactured entirely in Japan within a domestic GMP-certified factory, ensuring the highest standards of safety, purity, and processing control.

### VISUAL APPEARANCE:

* **Size:** Available in multi-size pouch options, including a standard 270-tablet bag (89.4g) and a compact 90-tablet trial size.
* **Shape:** Small, smooth, round dietary tablets specifically designed to be significantly smaller and much easier to swallow than traditional, bulky overseas vitamins.

* **How it is typically held or used:** The lightweight resealable pouch is easily held in one hand, allowing users to neatly dispense a few tablets at a time at home, or carry it in a bag for use at work.

### RESTRICTIONS:

* **No Medical Claims (PMD Act Compliance):** This product is a nutritional supplement and cannot be advertised to treat, cure, or medically prevent clinical edema (むくみ) or any other medical condition.
* **Dosage Management:** Users should adhere to the recommended daily intake guidelines and avoid excessive consumption; pregnant women, nursing mothers, or individuals under medication should consult a doctor before use.
* **Dietary Balance Reminder:** This supplement is intended to support an active lifestyle and should not be used as a total replacement for a varied, balanced diet and proper hydration.
