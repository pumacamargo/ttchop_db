**PRODUCT NAME:**
FOSMET QS60 Smartwatch (2026 Upgraded Bluetooth Calling Smart Watch)

**PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:**
The FOSMET QS60 is a premium rugged smartwatch that combines the appearance of a traditional luxury watch with modern smart features. Designed for everyday wear, fitness enthusiasts, and outdoor users, it offers Bluetooth calling, comprehensive health monitoring, sports tracking, customizable watch faces, and long battery life in a durable metal body with a stainless-steel band.

**HOW IT IS USED:**
The user pairs the smartwatch with an Android or iPhone using the companion mobile app via Bluetooth. Once connected, the watch can be used to make and receive calls, receive notifications, monitor health metrics, track workouts, control music, and access various daily tools. Users interact with the watch through the touchscreen and physical side buttons while wearing it on the wrist.

**MAIN BENEFITS:**

* Supports Bluetooth calling with a built-in microphone and speaker.
* Monitors heart rate continuously throughout the day.
* Tracks blood oxygen (SpO₂) levels for wellness awareness.
* Provides sleep monitoring and sleep quality analysis.
* Includes multiple sports modes for different exercise activities.
* Displays smartphone notifications, including calls, messages, and app alerts.
* Features customizable watch faces to match different styles.
* Offers weather updates, alarms, stopwatch, timers, and other useful tools.
* Compatible with both iPhone and Android devices.
* Long-lasting battery life suitable for several days of typical use.
* Durable metal construction with a premium stainless-steel strap.
* Includes magnetic charging for convenient recharging.

**VISUAL APPEARANCE:**

* **Size:** Large smartwatch face designed for easy viewing and operation.
* **Shape:** Circular display with a rugged, masculine design and pronounced bezel.
* **Color:** Primarily black and silver, featuring metallic finishes and dark accents.
* **Display:** Full-color touchscreen with bright graphics and multiple watch face options.
* **Band:** Stainless-steel link bracelet with adjustment tools included in the package.
* **How it is typically held or used:** Worn on the wrist like a conventional wristwatch and controlled through touch gestures and side buttons.

**RESTRICTIONS:**

* Bluetooth features require pairing with a compatible smartphone.
* Health measurements are intended for fitness and wellness purposes only and are not medical-grade diagnostic tools.
* Battery performance depends on usage habits, including calling frequency, screen brightness, and health-monitoring settings.
* Certain functions require the companion mobile application and appropriate permissions.
* Water resistance is intended for everyday situations; users should follow the manufacturer's recommendations regarding swimming, diving, and prolonged water exposure.
* Some notification features may vary depending on the smartphone operating system and app compatibility.
