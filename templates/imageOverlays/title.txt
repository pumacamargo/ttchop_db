# AI PRODUCT TITLE OVERLAY TEMPLATE

## PURPOSE

Generate a premium promotional graphic overlay featuring the exact product name as the primary visual element.

The overlay will later be composited over a video.

Its purpose is to instantly communicate the product name while creating a premium, attention-grabbing advertisement that looks professionally designed.

The product name is the single most important element and must remain perfectly accurate.

---

# CRITICAL TEXT ACCURACY (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

The product name will be provided exactly as written by the user.

This text MUST be reproduced character-for-character with absolute accuracy.

Treat the product name as immutable text.

Do NOT:

- Correct spelling
- Rewrite or paraphrase
- Translate any part
- Abbreviate words
- Omit words
- Reorder words
- Replace symbols
- Replace punctuation
- Replace numbers
- Invent missing characters
- Normalize spaces
- Change capitalization
- Substitute similar-looking Japanese, English, or Unicode characters

Every character—including spaces, capitalization, punctuation, Japanese characters, English text, numbers, units, symbols, trademarks, and special characters—must exactly match the provided input.

If the exact text cannot be rendered, regenerate the image rather than altering the text.

Before generating the final image, internally compare the rendered title against the provided product name and ensure they are identical.

Text accuracy is always more important than artistic styling.

---

# PRODUCT NAME INPUT

The product name will be inserted exactly as follows:

{{PRODUCT_NAME}}

Use this exact text as the main title.

Do not modify it in any way.

---

# TRANSPARENT BACKGROUND

Generate the overlay over a solid chroma green background (#00FF00).

Everything except the designed overlay elements should remain pure chroma green.

Do not add:

- Gradients
- Textures
- Shadows
- Lighting
- Particles
- Patterns

to the green background.

The background must remain perfectly flat for clean chroma key removal.

---

# GRAPHIC QUALITY

The overlay should look as though it was created by an experienced advertising graphic designer.

The typography itself should become a premium graphic element.

The final result should resemble high-end Japanese:

- TV commercials
- Variety show graphics
- TikTok Shop promotions
- Retail advertisements
- Product launch banners

The design should feel polished, expensive, and production-ready.

---

# TYPOGRAPHY STYLE

Transform the product name into premium advertising typography while preserving every character exactly.

Professional techniques may include:

- Thick outlines
- Double outlines
- Bevel effects
- Glossy finish
- Metallic accents
- Realistic highlights
- Soft reflections
- Subtle 3D extrusion
- Layered depth
- Inner shadows
- Outer shadows
- Dynamic perspective
- Slight curvature
- Gradient fills
- Premium embossed appearance

Do NOT excessively bend, stretch, compress, or distort any letters if doing so could reduce readability or alter the appearance of any character.

The product name must remain instantly readable.

---

Use bold, high-contrast advertising colors.

Recommended combinations include:

- White + Red
- Red + Yellow
- Gold + Black
- White + Blue
- Orange + Gold
- Yellow + Black

The chosen palette should maximize readability while creating visual excitement.

- Decorative objects covering text
- Overlapping letters

- Low contrast
- Tiny text

Readability always takes priority over artistic effects.

✓ No punctuation has changed.

✓ No spaces have changed.

✓ No capitalization has changed.

✓ No Japanese characters have changed.

✓ No English characters have changed.
