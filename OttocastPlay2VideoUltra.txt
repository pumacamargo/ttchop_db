Ottocast Play2Video Ultra is a compact CarPlay AI Box that connects to a vehicle's existing USB port and works through the car's original touchscreen and controls.

The device does not contain its own screen.

It allows drivers and passengers to enjoy video streaming and entertainment through the vehicle's existing dashboard display.

One of its primary advantages is that it enables access to popular video and entertainment services such as YouTube, Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, TV streaming services, and other supported content through the vehicle's existing display.

Many vehicles with standard Apple CarPlay or Android Auto do not allow video streaming playback while using their default systems. Ottocast expands the entertainment capabilities of the existing dashboard experience by making video content accessible through the vehicle's original screen and controls.

The main benefits are:

* access to video streaming and entertainment on the vehicle's existing display
* no need to reconnect a phone cable every trip
* cleaner dashboard setup
* quick plug-and-play setup
* entertainment during traffic, road trips, and breaks
* easy sharing of content with family and friends inside the car

The product is physically small, portable, and typically held in one hand.
