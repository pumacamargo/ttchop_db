**PRODUCT NAME:**
ARZOPA 16.1-inch Portable Monitor (FHD 1080P, 60Hz)

**PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:**
A slim and lightweight 16.1-inch portable monitor designed to expand your screen space while traveling, working remotely, gaming, or enjoying multimedia content. It features a Full HD IPS display with wide viewing angles and supports plug-and-play connectivity through USB-C and Mini HDMI. The monitor is compatible with laptops, gaming consoles, smartphones, and other supported devices.

**HOW IT IS USED:**
The user connects the monitor to a compatible device using a USB-C cable or Mini HDMI cable. It can function as a secondary display for productivity, an extended screen for presentations, or a portable gaming monitor for consoles such as the Nintendo Switch and PlayStation. The built-in kickstand allows users to position the screen at a comfortable viewing angle on a desk or tabletop.

**MAIN BENEFITS:**

* Expands workspace with a large 16.1-inch Full HD display.
* IPS panel provides vivid colors and wide viewing angles.
* Lightweight and portable design makes it easy to carry while traveling.
* Compatible with multiple devices, including laptops, smartphones, and gaming consoles.
* Plug-and-play setup with USB-C and Mini HDMI connectivity.
* Built-in stand allows convenient angle adjustment without additional accessories.

**VISUAL APPEARANCE:**

* **Size:** 16.1-inch display, approximately 19.1 mm thick and weighing around 0.78 kg.
* **Shape:** Ultra-slim rectangular monitor with thin bezels surrounding the screen.
* **Color:** Predominantly black with a minimalist and modern design.
* **How it is typically held or used:** Usually placed on a desk using the integrated kickstand, carried in a laptop bag for travel, or positioned beside a laptop as a dual-screen setup.

**RESTRICTIONS:**

* Does not include an internal battery and requires power from a connected device or external power source, depending on the setup.
* Touch functionality is not indicated for this model; it operates as a standard display monitor.
* Compatibility may vary depending on whether the connected device supports video output through USB-C (DisplayPort Alt Mode) or requires Mini HDMI connections.
